### waveforms config
| i2s param | dio pin | pico pin|
| --- | --- | --- |
|select | 0 | gp17 |

#### i2s logic setup
* bits - **16**  
* show - **right**  
* low - **right**
* sample - **rising**
* shift - **one**
* first - **MSB**
* align - **right**
* format - **two's compliment**
